## 15 Net-New Sources (not in V2/V3/V4/V5)

### Ranked by Impact

1. **NDWI Water Extent (Landsat via AppEEARS)** — Satellite surface water change. Where water appeared/disappeared this week. The waterfowl holy grail. ~50-200K entries.
2. **NPN First Frost / Green-up (usanpn.org)** — Phenological clock. First frost = migration start, green-up = turkey season. 45 years, ~100K entries.
3. **CPC Soil Moisture Anomalies (cpc.ncep.noaa.gov)** — Bridge between "it rained" and "there are ducks." Invisible water variable. Since 1948, ~400K entries.
4. **USGS Bird Banding Encounters (BBL)** — Actual origin-to-destination migration routes. Ground truth. ~1M encounter records for target species.
5. **NOAA Storm Events Database (ncdc.noaa.gov)** — 75 years of what happened after weather events. ~300K entries. Retrospective complement to live NWS alerts.
6. **USGS Earthquake Catalog (ComCat)** — Disaster thesis needs seismic. Animals detect foreshocks. Free API, back to 1900. ~50K entries (NA M3.0+).
7. **USDA FIA Tree Species / Mast Potential (fiadb.fs.usda.gov)** — #1 gap for deer/turkey. Mast drives everything. ~50K entries over 20 years.
8. **EPA Air Quality AQS (PM2.5/Ozone)** — Smoke redirects migration. Disaster co-signal. 4,000 stations, ~500K entries.
9. **NSIDC Lake Ice Phenology** — Ice-off = ducks arrive within 48 hours. 800 lakes, 100+ years, ~80K entries.
10. **NIFC Historical Fire Perimeters** — Habitat creation through succession. 40 years, ~100K perimeters.
11. **FAA Wildlife Strike Database** — Involuntary 24/7 census with altitude data. ~300K records since 1990.
12. **NOAA Coral Reef Watch SST Anomalies** — Gulf SST drives flyway moisture. Hurricane prediction. ~40K entries.
13. **USGS NAWQA Water Quality** — Nutrients drive food chain. Hidden variable for waterfowl. ~200K entries.
14. **Geomagnetic Kp Index (NOAA SWPC)** — Bird disorientation during magnetic storms. Since 1932, ~34K entries.
15. **CDC WNV Surveillance (ArboNET)** — Proxy for wetland conditions and predator dynamics. ~50K entries.

## Key Themes
- Water EXTENT (not just levels) is the biggest gap — NDWI fills it
- Phenological timing (NPN) converts raw weather into biological clock
- Soil moisture is the invisible bridge between precipitation and ecological response
- Banding data = actual migration routes = ground truth calibration for all other signals
- Seismic fills a total void in the disaster prediction thesis
- Mast production is the #1 deer/turkey signal with zero data in the brain
- The top 4 together enable genuinely PREDICTIVE statements about migration timing and location
