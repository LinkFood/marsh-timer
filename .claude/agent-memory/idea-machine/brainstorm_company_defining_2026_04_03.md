## Context
James declared DCD has pivoted to something that doesn't exist anywhere: a cross-domain historical data library in one vector space, indexed by date. 3.87M entries, 83+ domains, 1950-present. "The dataset IS the product." Asked for ideas that create an unbridgeable moat across 10 specific dimensions.

## Key Themes Identified
1. **Human cultural data IS the viral hook** -- sports scores, music charts, birthdays make people share
2. **The Date Card is the growth hack** -- shareable, personal, impossible to replicate elsewhere
3. **API + Reports = B2B revenue** -- B2C subscriptions are gravy; B2B licensing is the business
4. **The query loop must be VISIBLE** -- users need to see their fingerprint in the brain
5. **Coincidence Score is the credibility metric** -- co-occurrence vs random chance ratio
6. **The Grading Ledger is the trust engine** -- 29-0 record must be the most prominent thing on the site
7. **At 1B entries, resolution collapses to hyperlocal/hourly** -- causation becomes possible
8. **"Earthmind" captures both environment + intelligence** -- works as brand, product, and character
9. **The Birthday Report is the conversion funnel** -- nobody can NOT click it
10. **Embed Your Own Data = the product that sells itself** -- personal data + environmental data = discovery

## Data Sources Proposed (All Net-New vs Prior Recons)
### "The Human Record"
- Obituary archives (Legacy.com/newspapers.com)
- Congressional Record / Federal Register
- Billboard Hot 100 / music charts
- Every MLB/NFL/NBA/NHL game score (Retrosheet etc)
- AP/Reuters wire archive
- NASA APOD
- US Patent filings by date
- Volcano eruptions (Smithsonian GVP)
- Landsat imagery METADATA (not pixels)
- Wikipedia "On This Day" + Wikidata structured events

### "The Invisible Web"
- Radiocarbon calibration curve (IntCal20)
- Tree ring chronologies (ITRDB)
- Ice core records (NOAA Paleo)
- Sunspot observations (SILSO) -- 277 years daily
- Lightning strike data (GLD360/NLDN)
